// THE PROBLEM

Companies are flying blind
on AI compliance.

Every AI agent your organization runs is making autonomous decisions right now — with zero documentation, zero oversight records, and zero regulatory evidence.

⚖️

EU AI Act is Law

High-risk AI systems must have audit trails, human oversight records, and compliance documentation by August 2026. Most companies have nothing.

🇮🇳

DPDP Act 2023 Enforced

India's Data Protection Board is active. Rules notified November 2025. Maximum penalty Rs 250 crores per breach. AI decisions involving personal data are exposed.

🔍

Zero Audit Tooling

No standardized tooling exists at the agent-decision level. Legal teams are demanding governance. Boards are demanding oversight records. Nobody has answers.

// HOW IT WORKS

Three layers. Zero friction.

CompliancePilot wraps around your existing AI agents. No changes to your code. One import line and every decision is governed.

01

Decision Interception

A lightweight wrapper captures every AI agent decision — full input, output, tool calls, timestamps — before it reaches the end user. Zero latency impact.

02

Regulatory Classification

Gemini 2.5 Flash classifies each decision against EU AI Act, HIPAA, SOC 2, and DPDP Act 2023. Risk tier assigned in under 5 seconds. PII sanitized before any external API call.

03

Compliance Evidence

Auto-generates AI System Card, Decision Audit Trail, Human Oversight Record, and Risk Assessment Summary. Exportable as PDF on demand.

// FEATURES

Everything a compliance
team needs.

🛡️

PII Protection Layer

Zero raw personal data ever leaves the system. Aadhaar, PAN, phone numbers, emails — all masked before classification. Satisfies GDPR Article 25 and DPDP Act Section 8.

📊

Real-Time Risk Dashboard

Live decision feed with color-coded risk levels. Risk distribution charts. Pending review queue. Auto-refreshes every 10 seconds.

👁️

Human Review Workflow

Critical and High risk decisions automatically held in pending queue. Reviewer approves or rejects with justification. Full audit trail of every review action.

📄

Auto PDF Reports

One click generates a professional compliance report — AI System Card, Audit Trail, Oversight Record, Risk Summary. Ready for regulators and legal teams.

🔌

Multi-Agent Support

Monitor multiple AI agents simultaneously. Each agent identified by unique ID. Dashboard filterable by agent. Plug-and-play SDK — one import line.

⚡

Immutable Audit Logs

All decision logs are write-once. No entry can be edited or deleted. Preserves complete chain of custody for regulatory submissions.
